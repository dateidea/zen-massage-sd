Every image referenced in the build lives at `/public/images/<id>.jpg`.

**Business reference:** ViVi Sunshine Spa, 7151 El Cajon Blvd, Suite K,
San Diego, CA. Phone (858) 241-4343. Open daily 9 AM – 11 PM. Services:
Swedish, deep tissue, hot oil, Thai (and reflexology on request). New
clients save 20% on any 60-minute session.

---

**House rule (non-negotiable):** **NO PEOPLE in any frame.** Every image
is rooms, fabric, ceramics, hands-only details, plants, light through
windows. If a frame requires a body part to communicate the service, it
is a hand, forearm, or foot only — never a face, never a torso, never a
recognizable person.

**Shared style language to reuse across every prompt:**

> Photorealistic editorial photograph for a premium neighborhood massage
> studio website. Lighting: natural, soft, directional, late-afternoon
> golden warmth, slight film grain, gentle shallow depth of field. Color
> palette: muted, desaturated, warm neutrals — off-white (#F7F3EC),
> warm charcoal (#1C1A17), terracotta clay accent (#B8754A). No
> oversaturation, no HDR look, no AI-glossy rendering. No text, no
> logos, no watermarks. Absolutely no people in frame. If hands are
> required, hands must be anatomically correct, fingernails short and
> clean, no rings or jewelry. Shot on a 35mm or 50mm full-frame lens.
